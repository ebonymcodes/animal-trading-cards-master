Axolotl

Axolotls can regrow almost any part of their body, including their brains.

  • Scientific Name: Ambystoma Mexicanum
  • Average Length: About 9 Inches
  • Average Life Span: 10-15 Years
  • Habitat: Still Water Lakes

Life span for Axolotls greatly depend on any threats to its habitat. They are endangered due to human development, waste water disposal and habitat loss from droughts. However, its ability to regenerate can lenthen its life span.

Tiger Salamander

Courtship dances consist of very rapid head-tail circling, with salamanders touching side-side, noses against each other's hips.

  • Scientific Name: Ambystoma Tigrinum
  • Average Length: About 8 Inches
  • Average Life Span: 14 Years
  • Habitat: Underground Burrows

Typically, tiger salamander larvae feed and grow during the spring and early summer and metamorphose 2 to 5 months after hatching. However, some populations never metamorphose.
